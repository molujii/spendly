Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed version of your password. As you use Spendly, we store the expense records you create — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and enabling filters. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorized access. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third parties. We use Google Fonts to load typography — this involves a request to Google's servers but does not include any of your account or expense data.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or would like to request deletion of your data, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
